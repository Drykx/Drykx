## About Me

I’m passionate about **Artificial Intelligence**, with a focus on **Reinforcement Learning** and **Natural Language Processing**.  
My research explores improving the **robustness** and **generalization** of modern models — progressing toward **human-level reasoning**.

Beyond theory, I aim to design intelligent systems that **assist people meaningfully** and **benefit society**.

I have a solid foundation in **pure and applied mathematics**, particularly in:
- Probability & Statistics  
- Stochastic Processes  
- Measure Theory & Functional Analysis  

## Recent Projects

#### [RL Rumble](https://github.com/Drykx/RL-Rumble)
Implemented key **reinforcement learning** algorithms: **PPO**, **VFE**, and **SAC**, from scratch and benchmarked them across multiple environments.  
*Personal Project · May–June 2025*

#### [GPT-2 from Scratch](https://github.com/Drykx/GPT2-Scratch)
Rebuilt **GPT-2** architecture from the ground up to deeply understand **transformers**, and trained it on a poetry dataset. <br/>
*Personal Project · March–April 2025*

#### [Electricity Demand Forecasting](https://github.com/Drykx/Hackathon2025-ETH)
Predicted monthly electricity demand for thousands of consumers using **LightGBM** and advanced **feature engineering**.  
*ETH Hackathon · 4th/11 · April 2025*

#### [PerceptARC](https://github.com/Drykx/PerceptARC)
Developed a **mathematical and perceptual framework** for **ARC-AGI**. Proposing a classification-based abstraction approach inspired by human reasoning.  
*EPFL Semester Project (10 ECTS) · Sep 2024–Jan 2025*
